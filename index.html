Frequently Asked Questions

PetMe is a platform to cover all your petcare needs & provide you with seemless experience.

Can you trust CareTakers?

At PetME, we know it can be hard to entrust your pet’s welfare to someone else. After all, they’re not just a dog, cat, puppy – they’re a part of your family. It’s for this reason that Mad Paws takes Pet Sitting so seriously.

All of our CareTaker undergo a thorough verification procedure before they appear on PetMe.If you’re still not sure about a CareTaker, you can also read reviews from previous clients. By doing so, you can learn more about their pet care style from other Pet Owners.

Finally, when you book with PetMe Pet Care Service, your CareTaker doesn’t get paid until you confirm the end of Service. This gives you the opportunity to first assure that your pet is secure and happy before the CareTaker receives payment.

How to become CareTaker?

Are you interested in knowing how to become a certified Pet CareTaker? To begin the process, all you need to do is create a PetMe CareTaker account. You can do this by clicking “Become a CareTaker” in the top right corner under Account Section of the home page.

From there, we will guide you through our online training program and four-step verification process. After you’ve successfully completed this, you’ll be able to create a Pet Sitting profile, which will allow you to offer Dog Walking and many other services.
